Engineering Formulae

Radius Formulae

Formula:

\( R_1 = R_2 \cdot \cos(\text{angle}) + \sqrt{R_3^2 - R_2^2 \cdot \sin(\text{angle})^2} \)

Velocity Formulae

Formula:

\( V_1 = - W_2 \cdot R_2 \cdot \left[\sin(\text{angle}) + \frac{R_2 \cdot \sin(\text{angle}) \cdot \cos(\text{angle})}{\sqrt{R_3^2 - R_2^2 \cdot \sin(\text{angle})^2}}\right] \)
